Frequently Asked Questions

What are the limitations of Headroom Assistant on the Free plan?

On the Free plan, you can use Headroom Assistant for up to 2 transcriptions and 20 AI generation requests per device each month.

What happens after I subscribe?

You get instant access to all pro features. Your subscription will renew automatically based on the plan you selected.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access will remain until the current billing period ends.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! When you choose the Yearly Subscription, you get 1 week free before you’re charged. You can cancel anytime during the trial and you won’t be billed.

Why is the price different in my country?

Prices on the App Store are set in Euros, and Apple automatically adjusts the final amount based on your local currency, taxes, and regional pricing policies. We don’t manually set prices for each country. To see the exact price in your region, please check Headroom’s page on the App Store.
